# TradeLoop — Investor Pitch

## One Line
TradeLoop tells traders exactly how much money their bad habits cost them — in dollars, with proof.

## The Problem
95% of day traders lose money. Not because they can't trade — because they can't see their own behavioral patterns. They revenge trade after losses, overtrade on Fridays, size up after wins, and don't know which hours actually work. They FEEL like they know, but the data tells a different story.

Existing tools (TradeZella, Tradervue) show statistics. "You revenge traded 12 times." That's noise. Numbers without dollar impact don't change behavior. **80% of traders who start journaling quit within 2 weeks** because journals show data, not behavior change.

## The Solution
TradeLoop is the first trading behavior engine that computes the **exact dollar cost** of every behavioral mistake:

- "Revenge trading cost you exactly **$4,873**. Here's your equity curve without those trades."
- "Your unprofitable hours (9am, 11am) cost you **$12,188**. Your edge exists only between 10:00-10:30."
- "After 3+ loss streaks, your next trade wins **23%** of the time. You should stop."

Each insight includes: the specific pattern, the dollar impact, a what-if equity curve, and a specific recommendation.

## Product (Live)
- **Counterfactual Engine**: 8 behavioral leak detectors with dollar-value impact + what-if equity curves
- **Trade Replay**: Click any trade, see it on historical price charts with MFE/MAE analysis
- **Prop Firm Compliance**: Real-time drawdown tracking for FTMO, FundingPips, The5ers (5 presets)
- **Emotion Tracking**: Tag trades with mood, see "FOMO trades lose 3x more than calm trades"

- **Weekly Intelligence**: A-F graded report with specific action items
